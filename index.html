Hello everyone, I'm Fatema.To some, philosophy is considered to be one of the most mundane topic. This, most commonly, is due to the “unanswerable” questions which instil rage in the brains of students. Moreover, the idea that philosophy is useless is because people believe that it has nothing to do with the real world. To these people I would simply say this philosophy has everything to do with the real world. It is, in my opinion, a topic which solely focuses on the lives of humans and aims to answer questions or improve life.
